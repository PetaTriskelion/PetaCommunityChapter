About Us

The Peta Community Chapter of Tau Gamma Phi is committed to unity, brotherhood, leadership, and community service. We actively conduct feeding programs, outreach activities, and various community programs to support and uplift our local community.

History of Tau Gamma Phi

The Tau Gamma Phi (Triskelions' Grand Fraternity) was founded on October 4, 1968, at the University of the Philippines, Diliman, Quezon City. It was established to introduce a more democratic and less elitist brotherhood system focused on unity, equality, leadership, and adherence to the Tenets and Codes of Conduct.

While initially established by six student leaders, the fraternity commonly recognizes four main founding fathers, often referring to them collectively as part of the “Los Seis” or Six Founders.

The Six Founders (Los Seis)

• Rodolfo “Rod” Sta. Maria Confessor

• Roy Alolor Ordinario

• Talek (Hamias) Pablo

• Vedasto “Tito” Sario Venida

• Restituto “Rusty” Alfonso

• Romeo Fortes

Historical sources often emphasize the first four (Confessor, Ordinario, Pablo, and Venida) as key figures in the formal establishment of Tau Gamma Phi.
